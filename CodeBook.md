    * Integer 1:30
2. activity
    * Character string descrption of the activity being performed during the measurement period.
    * Character string within the set:

        * WALKING_UPSTAIRS
        * WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
        * SITTING
        * STANDING
        * LAYING
